PoolDex Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

PoolDex is a pool chemistry calculator built and maintained by Kevin Delawder. This policy explains what information the app handles. The short version: PoolDex does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

What data the app stores

PoolDex saves the following information only on your device, in your browser or app's local storage:

  • Pool configuration you enter (volume, sanitizer type, surface, etc.)
  • Test results you log (chlorine, pH, alkalinity, calcium, CYA, salt, temperature)
  • App preferences (language, units, theme)

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server, cloud account, or third party.

What data the app does not collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, address)
  • No location data
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers
  • No crash reporting that includes personal data
  • No account or sign-in is required to use the app

Permissions

PoolDex does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or files. It runs entirely offline after install.

Third-party services

PoolDex does not include any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The app may, in future versions, use Google Play Billing to process optional in-app purchases. If that changes, this policy will be updated and you will be notified in-app.

Children's privacy

PoolDex is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, regardless of age.

Deleting your data

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can erase everything by uninstalling the app or by clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and a notice will appear in the app.
